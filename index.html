I'm a Multimedia Artist !


Throughout my academic career, I have had the opportunity to work on many forms of media, ranging from traditional to digital mediums.

As a multimedia artist, my goal is to not only incorporate and support the aesthetics of an idea, but to also ensure that the idea can be effectively communicated to, and understood, by the intended audience.

Graphic Design

Process Binders

My time at Arizona State University enabled me to work on a variety of projects simultaneously and in a timely manner. The work I was tasked with challenged me to see things in ways that would require me to hone in on my attention to detail, and recognize that there are many factors that go into a balanced design, from color theory to topography. Trial and error became a routine that pushed for both my peers and I to grow as future designers in a fast-paced environment.


Magazines

While I worked on most projects independently throughout my academic career, collaborative work was also to be expected in a setting that often requires an exchange of knowledge and distribution of work. Some of these works included designing the layouts and managing the content that goes into the magazines. This means that while the overall look and feel of a work is crucial from a design perspective, it is important to design from the mind of the audience consuming the product, as well.
