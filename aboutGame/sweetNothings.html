Play Sweet Nothings

Experience

This game is a product of two-semesters-worth of production. As the lead artist on my team, I was given the task of producing all the arts and assets for the game, ranging from concept art, character sprites, background design, and UI design.

This role served as a reminder that as much as aesthetics can define a product, the ability to communicate to the audience and allow the audience to engage with the product seamlessly is just as important.

Along the way, I was reminded of the importance of how much a strong bond between a group can provide a strong foundation for work, as well.

Responsibilities:

  • Collaborate effectively and efficiently with team members by establishing roles and ensuring all tasks are fulfilled in a timely and well-ordered workflow
  • Design UI assets that allow players to navigate and add input into the game, ensuring that UI is clear and concise for players to comprehend the information being presented
  • Design concept arts and assets in a timely manner that would be imported into games, making sure that all assets and designs would be accessible amongst team members
  • Ensure artistic consistency across the game
  • Utilize content management and design software to design, and maintain website content, graphics, and landing pages as well as monitoring adherence to compliance standards to market game
