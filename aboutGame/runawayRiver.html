Runaway River

2022

Runaway River is a single player, 3D, arcade-style infinite runner where the player must navigate their way through treacherous terrain tethered to a rampaging giant fish by a fishing line!

Play RUNAWAY RIVER

Experience

This was one of my games that I helped design for a course's final project. As a team, we managed to divide work amongst everyone under a designated role.

My role as the lead artist meant that I was to provide assets for the game, which meant being able to model, rig, and animate objects, plan and create UI layouts, and overseeing the overall artistic side of the game, all in due time.

As this game was the first time I learned how to 3D model, rig, and animate, I learned a lot along the way by adopting to the programs I was required to use for the project.

Responsibilities:

  • Collaborate effectively and efficiently with team members by establishing roles and ensuring all tasks are fulfilled in a timely and well-ordered workflow
  • Design UI assets that allow players to navigate and add input into the game, ensuring that UI is clear and concise for players to comprehend the information being presented
  • Design concept arts and 3D-modeled assets in a timely manner that would be imported into games, making sure that all assets and designs would be accessible amongst team members
  • Ensure artistic consistency across the game
  • Utilize content management and design software to design, and maintain website content, graphics, and landing pages as well as monitoring adherence to compliance standards to market game
