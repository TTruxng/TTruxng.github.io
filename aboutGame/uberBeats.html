Experience

Ubér Beats was a product of a game jam that I worked on over the summer with a group I had met in one of my game design courses.

As this was the first time I was not the sole artist of a game, this project emphasized the importance of having a strong and communicative team, along with the flexibility to make changes under time constraints.

Moreover, because I was able to work with one other artist, the time we spent exploring new ideas and executing them, along with receiving criticism from not just each other, but the rest of the team, too, made this experience all the more valuable.

Responsibilities:

  • Collaborate effectively and efficiently with team members by establishing roles and ensuring all tasks are fulfilled in a timely and well-ordered workflow
  • Design concept arts and assets in a timely manner that would be imported into games, making sure that all assets and designs would be accessible amongst team members
  • Ensure artistic consistency across the game
  • Utilize content management and design software to design, and maintain website content, graphics, and landing pages as well as monitoring adherence to compliance standards to market game
